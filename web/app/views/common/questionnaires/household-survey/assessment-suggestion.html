Weed control
Sustainability Indicators: Weed control
Farmers current practices: Both manual and herbicide application Herbicide application Manual application
Next season suggestions:

The weed infestation in your field is high. You may consider to apply pre- or post-emergence herbicide in the next season rice.

Please consider application of herbicides less than 4 times during the crop growing period. Apply reliable good quality herbicide and keep the field moist (saturated) during the time of herbicide application.

The weed control could be in-efficient, please try to create weed free condition at the time of crop establishment and make better water management in next season.

Please consider application of herbicide within 40 days after crop establishment. If weed infestation exists after 40 days after crop establishment, you could consider using manual labor. Early period weed free (keep crop weed free within 40 days) is very important for the better yield of the crop.

Besides above points, for sustainably increasing rice production and net profits, please also consider the following point in the next season:
  • You consider to use high yielding inbred or hybrid varieties.
  • You consider to use certified seed.
  • Please try to use nursery seedling of 14-22 days old.
  • You may consider using lower seed rate. In this season, the seed rate which you used seems too high which increased cost of production too.
  • Please consider application of fertilizer.
  • Please consider application of irrigation, if the crop was suffered due to drought, please irrigate at least during panicle initiation, flowering and milking stages and at the time of fertilizer application.
  • Please consider timely threshing of rice. Drying of un-threshed rice for very long period could increase the post-harvest loss and decrease rice grain quality.
